Steak and Chips Recipe

Designed by Freepik

Recipe Description

Steak and chips is a classic dish that perfectly balances the rich, savory flavors of a juicy, tender steak with the crispy, golden texture of freshly cooked fries. The steak, often seasoned simply with salt and pepper, is grilled or pan-seared to perfection, allowing its natural flavors to shine through. The accompanying chips, whether thick-cut or shoestring, are fried until crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside, providing a delightful contrast to the succulent meat. This dish is often served with a side of tangy ketchup or a creamy dipping sauce, enhancing the overall experience.

Choosing steak for this dish adds a hearty and indulgent element that elevates the meal to a satisfying comfort food experience. The beef's robust flavor profile pairs wonderfully with the simplicity of the chips, creating a harmonious blend of textures and tastes. Whether enjoyed as a casual meal or a special treat, steak and chips offer a timeless combination that appeals to meat lovers and food enthusiasts alike.

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks (about 1 inch thick)
  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into fries
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • Ketchup or your favorite dipping sauce (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Spread the cut potatoes on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika. Toss to coat evenly. Bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden and crispy.
  2. While the fries are baking, season the steaks generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
  3. Heat a grill pan or skillet over high heat. Once hot, add the steaks and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare, or longer if you prefer your steak more well-done. Remove from heat and let rest for a few minutes.
  4. Serve the steaks alongside the crispy fries, with ketchup or your favorite dipping sauce on the side if desired.
  5. Enjoy your delicious steak and chips!
