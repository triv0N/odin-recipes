Chicken Fajitas Recipe

Designed by Freepik

Recipe Description

Chicken fajitas are a vibrant, crowd-pleasing dish that brings together tender strips of marinated chicken, colorful bell peppers, and onions, all sizzling hot from the pan. The secret lies in the marinade — a mix of lime, garlic, and spices that infuse the chicken with bold, zesty flavor. Served with warm tortillas, a touch of sour cream, and fresh salsa or guacamole, chicken fajitas offer the perfect balance of smoky, tangy, and savory elements in every bite.

Choosing chicken over beef or pork gives fajitas a lighter, fresher character without losing their satisfying richness. The lean meat absorbs the spices beautifully, creating a dish that feels both hearty and wholesome. Combined with crisp vegetables and bright toppings, chicken fajitas deliver that comforting Tex-Mex warmth while keeping things balanced, flavorful, and endlessly adaptable to personal taste.

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • Sour cream, salsa, and guacamole for serving (optional)

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, chili powder, ground cumin, paprika, salt, black pepper, and lime juice. Mix well to create the marinade.
  2. Add the sliced chicken breasts to the marinade, ensuring each piece is well coated. Let it marinate for at least 15 minutes (or up to 2 hours in the refrigerator for more flavor).
  3. Heat a large skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the marinated chicken slices. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add a little more olive oil if needed. Add the sliced bell peppers and onions. Sauté for about 5-6 minutes until they are tender-crisp and slightly caramelized.
  5. Return the cooked chicken to the pan with the vegetables. Stir everything together and cook for an additional 2 minutes to combine the flavors.
  6. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  7. To serve, spoon the chicken and vegetable mixture onto each tortilla. Add sour cream, salsa, and guacamole if desired.
  8. Roll up the tortillas and enjoy your delicious chicken fajitas!
